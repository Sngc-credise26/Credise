General Instructions

  • CREDISE–26 is a one day inter collegiate arts festival comprising various Cultural, Literary and Fine Arts competitions
  • Attractive cash prizes are at stake. Spot registration commences by 9.00 AM and online registration can be done through the link given
  • ID Cards & Bonafide Certificates are compulsory for participation
  • Limited unfurnished AC accommodation will be provided for both boys and girls on a first come first serve basis
  • Food will be provided for the participants & teachers
  • Participants are requested to take care of their belongings as the organizers will not be responsible for any loss or damage
  • Only participants & staff in-charge will be permitted inside the college. Guests will not be entertained
  • Participants should bring a CD mentioning the track number and the name of the college
  • Fire and Snake dances are strictly prohibited
  • Decision of judges will be final and binding. Transportation will be arranged on prior intimation from selected destinations of Palakkad and Coimbatore
  • If the number of participants per team is less than 3 for any event, the event will be cancelled
  • Unruly behaviour within the campus will lead to disqualification
  • Organizers reserve the right to change the rules and regulations of any event without prior notice

Credise is the flagship intercollegiate cultural fest of Sree Narayana Guru College. The fest brings together creativity, talent and youthful energy through diverse cultural, literary and fine arts events conducted over two vibrant days.
